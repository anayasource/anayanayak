Avocado Sushi

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • seweed rap
  • sticky rice
  • Green chillies

Instructions

  1. First get your hands wet so the rice will stick
  2. Put seaweed rap on the table and put it flat
  3. then you put the rice on the seaweed rap
  4. do the same with you topings I picked Avocado and Green chillies for mine
  5. then you roll it
  6. and you say Yummy!
